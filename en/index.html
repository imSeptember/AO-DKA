Welcome to Law Firm "Dniprovsky Bar Association of Kyiv City"

Law Firm "Dniprovsky Bar Association of Kyiv City" is one of the oldest Ukrainian bar associations, which recently celebrated its 30th anniversary. It is the cradle of the legal profession in Kyiv and Ukraine.

The association is a non-governmental self-governing institution that ensures the implementation of a high standard of protection, representation, and provision of other types of legal assistance with unwavering adherence to the norms of professional ethics and advocacy mastery in conditions of constant development..

On this website, every visitor can thoroughly acquaint themselves with the areas of activity of the association. Additionally, there is an opportunity to submit a request for legal assistance. Thanks to this resource, a transparent mechanism of interaction with the public is created, providing legal support to everyone in need.
